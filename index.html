RMC MANUFACTURING UNIT

Shantilata Group's RMC Manufacturing Unit is a state-of-the-art facility dedicated to the production of high-quality Ready-Mixed Concrete (RMC). With a team of experienced engineers and technicians, we utilize the latest technology and advanced manufacturing processes to ensure consistent and reliable concrete products.

We are the authorized manufacturer of ready mix Concrete for Ultratech, Odisha.

UltraTech was the first RMC company in India to adopt the 'baton wash' technology. This is being used in more than sixty recycling plants in its RMC operations across India.

At Shantilata Group, we take pride in our commitment to safety and environmental sustainability. Our manufacturing processes are designed to minimize waste and ensure a safe working environment for our employees and the community.
